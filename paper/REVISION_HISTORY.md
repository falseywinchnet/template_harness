# Revision history

## Unreleased

- Status: active draft
- Target version: `TARGET-v1`

- Evidence changes: none recorded
- Compatibility: not yet frozen

For each public release, record version, date, commit, tag, archival identity,
claim changes, evidence changes, and whether the earlier release remains
scientifically valid. Never rewrite a released entry in place.

## Change classification

Classify proposed edits before changing a submitted or released manuscript:

- **editorial** — wording or layout only;
- **strengthened presentation** — equivalent, shorter, or more explicit route;
- **evidence upgrade** — same claim with stronger replay, formal, or archival
  support;
- **scope correction** — an intermediate statement is narrowed to what was
  actually proved;
- **gap repair** — a missing justification is supplied;
- **claim change** — result, domain, assumptions, or conclusion changes;
- **corrigendum/retraction** — a public claim is false or materially misleading.

For every non-editorial change list the affected claim IDs and locations, old
and new evidence boundaries, downstream consistency audit, disclosure language,
and version action. A cleaner proof is not automatically a gap repair. A real
gap must not be described as polish. Preserve the earlier PDF, source, commit,
tag, date, and digest even when it remains scientifically valid.
