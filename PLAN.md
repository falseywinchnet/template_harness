# Project plan

This is the editable source for the project register. Tailor titles, tags,
dependencies, and acceptance gates after bootstrap, then run
`./h register PLAN.md`. Keep stable explicit IDs once work begins.

- [ ] P002 | Freeze scope, non-goals, deliverable, and audience | stage=scope | mode=refine | tags=charter,scope | depends=P001 | accept=Target and exclusions are explicit and approved
- [ ] P003 | Decompose the target into claims and dependency edges | stage=scope | mode=refine | tags=claims,dependencies | depends=P002 | accept=Every target claim has an ID, exact falsifier, object-identity and instantiation edges, initialization distinct from preservation, status, and immediate prerequisites
- [ ] P004 | Build the source and prior-work inventory | stage=sources | mode=refine | tags=sources,literature | depends=P002 | accept=Used sources are preserved or have an explicit acquisition status and locator
- [ ] P005 | Reproduce foundational results or baselines | stage=sources | mode=advance | tags=baseline,reproduction | depends=P004 | accept=Baseline procedure and observed output are recorded independently
- [ ] P006 | Select methods, representations, and compute budgets | stage=design | mode=refine | tags=methods,design,performance | depends=P003,P005 | accept=Each method records assumptions, failure tests, verification boundary, arithmetic model, scale, bounded stages of at most four local minutes, checkpointing, and a timeout pivot condition
- [ ] P007 | Execute the primary analysis or proof route | stage=analysis | mode=advance | tags=core,analysis | depends=P006 | accept=Primary route reaches a supported result or a preserved exact failure boundary
- [ ] P008 | Explore alternatives and preserve negative results | stage=analysis | mode=advance | tags=alternatives,failures | depends=P006 | accept=Material alternatives and failures are recorded without selection bias
- [ ] P009 | Convert surviving work into explicit evidence obligations | stage=verification | mode=refine | tags=evidence,claims | depends=P007,P008 | accept=Every surviving claim maps to a source, replay, formal proof, or unresolved obligation
- [ ] P010 | Close the critical argument or empirical analysis | stage=verification | mode=advance | tags=core,closure | depends=P009 | accept=Critical path has no hidden assumption, missing case, or unsupported inference
- [ ] P011 | Run independent falsification and adversarial tests | stage=verification | mode=refine | tags=falsification,audit | depends=P010 | accept=Predeclared counterexample conditions and boundary cases have been tested
- [ ] P012 | Formalize or certify the high-risk seam where justified | stage=verification | mode=either | tags=formal,certificate,performance | depends=P010 | accept=Chosen seam has a clean pinned build and axiom audit, matches the target bidirectionally, proves object identity, objective interior, actual-target instantiation, exact falsifier equivalence, and any initialization separately from preservation, and stays inside its build budget
- [ ] P013 | Audit claim-to-evidence closure and trusted base | stage=verification | mode=refine | tags=evidence,audit | depends=P011,P012 | accept=All reachable leaves are supported, trusted explicitly, or visibly unresolved
- [ ] P014 | Freeze the manuscript map and proof or analysis narrative | stage=writing | mode=refine | tags=paper,map | depends=P013 | accept=Public claim spine is identical in scope from title through release metadata; every section has a reader question, entry dependencies, one burden, an exit state, claim IDs, and evidence class; optional branches are cut or assigned deliberately
- [ ] P015 | Write the complete manuscript | stage=writing | mode=advance | tags=paper,writing | depends=P014 | accept=Draft follows reader dependency order, states the target and load-bearing transitions accurately, uses claim-calibrated rhetoric, separates theory from verification mechanics, and discloses concrete limitations
- [ ] P016 | Build the reproducibility package | stage=reproduction | mode=refine | tags=reproduction,release,compute | depends=P013 | accept=A clean environment can run the truthful quick audit and regenerate every theorem-bearing or result-bearing artifact through named bounded stages with recorded resources
- [ ] P017 | Conduct internal adversarial review | stage=review | mode=refine | tags=review,audit | depends=P015,P016 | accept=Separate claim, dependency, reader, literature, revision, and release passes cover statement fidelity, definition laundering, objective interior, initialization versus closure, exact falsifier, evidence language, novelty, limitations, captions, and public-spine consistency
- [ ] P018 | Resolve findings and freeze claims | stage=review | mode=refine | tags=review,freeze | depends=P017 | accept=Fatal and major findings are resolved or explicitly block release; remaining limitations are disclosed; claim statuses, manuscript map, and change classifications are frozen
- [ ] P019 | Package manuscript, metadata, checksums, and release manifest | stage=publication | mode=refine | tags=publication,package,pdf | depends=P018 | accept=Release package is minimal, deterministic, identified, independently checked, passes normal and release-mode text audits plus page-by-page PDF inspection, and agrees with the public claim spine and revision identity
- [ ] P020 | Publish and archive an immutable release | stage=publication | mode=refine | tags=publication,archive | depends=P019 | accept=Public artifact, source revision, archival identity, and citation metadata agree
